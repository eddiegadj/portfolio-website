Rentberry AI Agent (RAI)

Product design · UX/UI · AI experience

The Rentberry AI Agent is an intelligent rental assistant built for the U.S. market, helping renters navigate the housing search with simple, conversational interactions. I led the end-to-end product design — defining user flows, crafting the interface, and shaping how AI recommendations appear and behave.

The experience turns a traditionally complex rental process into a guided, personalized journey that surfaces the right listings faster and with less friction.
